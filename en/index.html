"Voice-Only" Event PA System Rental

This is suitable for "voice-only" event with less than 200 audience. Examples include talk, seminar, sharing session, background music for exhibition, promotion booth and etc.

These kind of events do not need too many equipments. However, the minimal charges for a PA system is not low. To take care of this situation, Hopeful Production has trimmed down the non-essential gears and help our client to keep down the cost.

In this package, 1 mixing system, 2 powered speakers, 1 wireless microphone and 1 technician will be included. For quotation, click here to contact us.

Indoor Event PA System Rental

This is suitable for indoor events with less than 400 participants. Examples include workshop, restaurant activity, annual dinner, wedding, pool side party, cocktail party and etc. Participants will generate volume like talking during the activity and higher volume is needed than “voice-only” situation.

Placement of equipments is important in these events. Technicians in Hopeful Audio Production will install speakers accordingly to the characteristics of venue. We will ensure a balanced listening level from the front to last rows. For more complicated venues like a L-shape one, we would be please to suggest other PA system package.

In this package, 1 mixing system, 4 powered speakers, 2 wireless microphone and 2 technician will be included. For quotation, click here to contact us.

Stage Event PA System Rental

This is suitable for stage event with less than 400 people. Examples include school open day, carnival, singing contest, talent show, variety show, outdoor performance and etc. If stage is involve in an event, a fold-back system is required to provide performers with comfortable monitoring and prevent feedback.

Hopeful Audio Production understands that our clients are not expertise in audio system. We will pay careful attention to your expectation and program rundown, in order to suggest proper gear setting and playback of sound. In addition, our latest remote mixing system allows monitoring and control from the best location regardless of restriction in venue.

In this package, 1 mixing system, 6 powered speakers, 4 wireless microphone and 2 technician will be included. For quotation, click here to contact us.
